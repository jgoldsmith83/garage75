Garage 75, located on James Island, is Charleston’s hottest spot for food, drink, billiards and games. We offer a clean, relaxing atmosphere where friends and family of all ages can come together to spend some quality fun time.

Stop by today and try any of our mouthwatering wings, burgers, sandwiches, salads and appetizers all while following your favorite sports teams. With over 20 televisions, you can always have YOUR game on. Featuring NFL, College Football, NCAA hoops, NHL hockey and Soccer from around the world.

If you’re in the mood for some fun, step into our game room and challenge your friends to a game of billiards , skee-ball, golden tee, ping pong, and dozens more.

Old school shuffleboarders will appreciate our 14 foot table and we ALWAYS have FREE darts and Ping-Pong.

We offer a full-service bar with a great selection of beers on tap and in the bottle that fit any taste and budget. If you prefer something with a little more kick, let one of our friendly bar staff mix up your favorite concoction.

If you’re looking for a good time and some good people, make the trek and see what all of the buzz is about. We look forward to serving you!
